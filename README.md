## Low-Code-No-Code-Langflow

**Enterprise-ready custom components and templates for [Langflow](https://langflow.org/), built with data privacy and security as the top priority.**

### Our Vision

This repository is building a family of robust, ready-to-use Langflow nodes and templates. Our mission is to empower corporates and other industries to integrate powerful AI workflows **without being afraid of data leaks** or compromising sensitive company information.

We believe in secure, on-premise AI. These components are designed to operate entirely within your own infrastructure, giving you full control over your data.

### Who is this for?

  * **IT/Security Teams** wanting to provide a safe "AI sandbox" for their company.
  * **Data Science & Engineering Teams** building internal applications on sensitive corporate data.
  * **Corporates** in regulated industries (finance, healthcare, legal) that cannot risk third-party data exposure.
